Inventory optimization and strategy with Spirit of '76:

I worked with Spirit of '76 in the Summer of 2022. It was a unique time for the industry due to covid-19. In 2020, sales were at a near 5 year low. In 2021, sales reached a record high, and in 2022, we didn't know what to expect. Planning during this period was difficult. We had expectations that sales would be higher than the previous year but only through careful planning. I worked with the Tent Manager to provide data-driven inventory orders with a few constraints. The focus was to optimize profit. In certain product categories we needed to minimize space prodcut took up. In others, we had to ensure we could sell enough of the high priced items to justify the cost.
